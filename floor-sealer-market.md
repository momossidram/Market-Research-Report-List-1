<p><strong>The Floor Sealer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Polyurethane</li><li>Solvent-based</li><li>Water-based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The floor sealer market is categorized into three main types: polyurethane, solvent-based, and water-based sealers. Polyurethane sealers offer exceptional durability and abrasion resistance, suitable for high-traffic areas. Solvent-based sealers provide a strong protective layer and are ideal for outdoor applications due to their resistance to weather conditions, but they emit volatile organic compounds. Water-based sealers are low in odor and environmentally friendly, making them suitable for indoor use, while still providing a decent level of protection.</p></p>
