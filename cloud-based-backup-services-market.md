<p><p>Cloud-based backup services can be categorized into three market types: public, private, and hybrid clouds. Public cloud services offer scalable storage and are managed by third-party providers, making them cost-effective for businesses seeking flexibility. Private clouds provide dedicated infrastructure for a single organization, offering enhanced security and control, ideal for those with strict compliance needs. Hybrid clouds combine both public and private cloud features, enabling businesses to benefit from both cost efficiency and high security, optimizing their backup strategy.</p></p>
