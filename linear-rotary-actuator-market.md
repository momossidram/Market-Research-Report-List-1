<p><p>The linear rotary actuator market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and electric actuators. Pneumatic actuators use compressed air for rapid, lightweight operations, ideal for applications needing quick movements with minimal force. Hydraulic actuators leverage fluid pressure, providing high force capabilities for heavy-duty tasks in industrial settings. Electric actuators utilize electrical energy to achieve precise positioning and control, making them suitable for applications requiring accuracy and reliability. Each type serves specific needs based on performance, power source, and application requirements.</p></p>
