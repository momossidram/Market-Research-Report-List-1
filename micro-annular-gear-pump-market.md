<p><strong>The Micro Annular Gear Pump Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>External Gear Pump</li><li>Internal Gear Pump</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro annular gear pump market features two primary types: external gear pumps and internal gear pumps. External gear pumps consist of two interlocking gears that create a specific flow path, making them ideal for high-viscosity fluids. Internal gear pumps, characterized by a gear mechanism where one gear rotates within another, offer a smoother and quieter operation, suitable for lower-viscosity applications. Both types are crucial in various industries, providing efficient fluid transfer solutions tailored to specific operational needs.</p></p>
