<p><p>The Auxiliary Heating System market is primarily categorized into two main types: fuel-based auxiliary heaters and electrical/PTC auxiliary heaters. Fuel-based auxiliary heaters utilize fossil fuels, such as diesel or natural gas, providing efficient heating solutions for larger applications. In contrast, electrical or PTC (Positive Temperature Coefficient) auxiliary heaters rely on electric power and are favored for their quick response time and compact design. Both types are essential for enhancing heating efficiency in diverse settings, including residential and commercial environments.</p></p>
