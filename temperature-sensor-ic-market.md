<p><ul><li>Analog Temperature Sensor IC</li><li>Digital Temperature Sensor IC</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Temperature Sensor IC market is divided into two main types: Analog and Digital Temperature Sensor ICs. Analog Temperature Sensor ICs provide continuous voltage or current signals corresponding to temperature changes, suitable for applications requiring real-time monitoring. In contrast, Digital Temperature Sensor ICs output temperature readings in a digital format, typically through protocols like I2C or SPI, offering higher accuracy and ease of integration with microcontrollers. Each type serves distinct applications across industries like automotive, healthcare, and consumer electronics.</p></p>
