<p><ul><li>Spiral Weld Pipe</li><li>Electric Resistance Weld</li><li>Double Submerged Arc Weld</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cold rolled sheet piles market encompasses various manufacturing methods, including spiral weld pipe, electric resistance weld, and double submerged arc weld. Spiral weld pipe involves the helical welding of steel strips, offering high strength and flexibility for various applications. Electric resistance weld utilizes electric currents to bond edges of steel sheets, ensuring a strong and uniform seam. Double submerged arc weld provides robust joints by simultaneously welding from both sides, ideal for heavy-duty structures requiring exceptional durability and integrity.</p></p>
