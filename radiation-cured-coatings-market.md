<p><p>Radiation cured coatings encompass methods that utilize ultraviolet (UV) light and electron beam (EB) irradiation to cure coatings quickly and effectively. UV curing involves exposing photosensitive materials to UV light, triggering a chemical reaction that hardens the coating. Electron beam curing uses high-energy electron beams, providing deeper penetration and thorough curing without the need for solvents. Both methods offer benefits such as faster production times, reduced volatile organic compound emissions, and improved durability in various applications, including automotive and industrial coatings.</p></p>
