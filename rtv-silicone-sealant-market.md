<p><p>RTV (Room Temperature Vulcanizing) silicone sealant is available in two main types: single component and double component. Single component silicone sealant is ready to use and cures when exposed to moisture in the air, making it convenient for general applications like sealing windows and doors. In contrast, double component silicone sealant requires mixing two components before application, offering enhanced durability and resistance to extreme conditions, making it ideal for industrial uses and environments demanding superior performance.</p></p>
