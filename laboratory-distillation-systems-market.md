<p><p>The laboratory distillation systems market is categorized into several types, each serving specific applications. Distillation involves separating liquids based on boiling points, ideal for purifying chemicals. Steam distillation utilizes steam to extract volatile compounds, often used in essential oil production. Vacuum distillation lowers pressure to enable distillation at lower temperatures, beneficial for sensitive materials. Fractional distillation separates a mixture into its components, effectively refining complex mixtures such as petroleum. These methods cater to diverse industries, enhancing product quality and efficiency.</p></p>
