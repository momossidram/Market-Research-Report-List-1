<p><p>The Tiles Parquet Floors market primarily consists of two types: engineered and solid parquet flooring. Engineered parquet is made from layered wood, providing stability and resistance to moisture, making it suitable for various environments. It typically features a hardwood veneer layer on top for an authentic appearance. Solid parquet, on the other hand, consists of single pieces of hardwood, offering durability and the potential for refinishing. Both types cater to different consumer preferences for aesthetics, installation, and maintenance.</p></p>
