<p><strong>The 5G mmWave Small Cell Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Integrated Small Cell</li><li>Distributed Small Cell</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The 5G mmWave Small Cell market is divided into two main types: integrated small cells and distributed small cells. Integrated small cells combine radio and antenna elements into a single unit, facilitating easier deployment in urban areas. In contrast, distributed small cells involve a network of separated components, where the radio head is distanced from the antenna, allowing greater flexibility in placement and enhanced coverage. Both types aim to support high-capacity 5G networks, enhancing connectivity in densely populated regions.</p></p>
