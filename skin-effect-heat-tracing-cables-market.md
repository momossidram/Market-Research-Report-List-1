The market comprises two main types: medium temperature type, operating typically between 100°F and 300°F, suitable for applications like freeze protection or maintaining low-temperature processes; and high temperature type, exceeding 400°F, which is ideal for more extreme environments such as oil and gas pipelines, preserving fluid viscosity.</p></p>
