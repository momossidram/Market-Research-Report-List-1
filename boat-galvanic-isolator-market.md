<p><ul><li>Signal Isolation</li><li>Power Isolation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The boat galvanic isolator market is divided into two main types: signal isolation and power isolation. Signal isolation prevents corrosion by isolating electrical signals between the boat and shore power, ensuring safe communication without ground loops. Power isolation focuses on protecting the boat's electrical system by preventing stray currents from shore connections, thereby minimizing the risk of galvanic corrosion. Both types enhance safety and longevity of marine electrical systems, catering to the needs of boat owners and operators.</p></p>
