<p><p>The medical vacuum filters market features key types, including deep filters and cellulose membrane filters. Deep filters excel in capturing particles and contaminants by utilizing a thick bed of filter media, making them ideal for high-viscosity fluids. Cellulose membrane filters, on the other hand, are widely used for sterile filtration due to their ability to retain microorganisms while allowing liquids to pass through. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the safety and efficacy of medical applications.</p></p>
