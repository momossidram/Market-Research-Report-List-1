<p><p>The custom printed tapes market comprises primarily two types: double-sided tape and single-sided tape. Double-sided tape features adhesive on both sides, allowing it to bond two surfaces together discreetly, often used in crafting and packaging. Single-sided tape, on the other hand, has adhesive on one side, typically used for sealing, labeling, or bundling items. Both types can be customized with branding, logos, or designs, catering to various industries like packaging, construction, and retail, enhancing product visibility and functionality.</p></p>
