<p><ul><li>Bone Growth Stimulators (BGS)</li><li>Viscosupplements</li><li>Bone Graft Substitute</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The biological bone repair materials market comprises three main types: Bone Growth Stimulators (BGS), Viscosupplements, and Bone Graft Substitutes. Bone Growth Stimulators enhance the healing process in fractures by promoting cell proliferation and regeneration. Viscosupplements, primarily used for osteoarthritis, alleviate pain and improve joint function by increasing synovial fluid viscosity. Bone Graft Substitutes serve as alternatives to autologous grafts, providing scaffolding for new bone formation, and are crucial in orthopedic and dental applications for effective healing.</p></p>
