<p><p>The Mitral Valve Repair Interventional Devices Market comprises three main types: </p><p>1. **Transcatheter Edge-to-Edge Repair (TEER)** involves a minimally invasive approach to stitch the mitral valve leaflets together, enhancing valve function and reducing regurgitation.</p><p>2. **Transcatheter Mitral Annuloplasty** focuses on remodeling the annulus or ring of the mitral valve to improve its shape and support.</p><p>3. **Other** includes emerging technologies and devices that may offer innovative solutions for mitral valve repair beyond the established methods.</p></p>
