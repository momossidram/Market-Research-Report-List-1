<p><p>The electric rock drill market comprises primarily two types: pneumatic rock drills and hydraulic rock drills. Pneumatic rock drills use compressed air for operation, making them suitable for various drilling tasks, especially in remote locations without electricity. Hydraulic rock drills, on the other hand, leverage hydraulic power for more efficient and powerful drilling, often used in larger construction or mining projects where energy sources are available. Both types cater to specific needs in the construction and mining industries.</p></p>
