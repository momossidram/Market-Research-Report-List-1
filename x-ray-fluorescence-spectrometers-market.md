<p><p>The X-ray Fluorescence Spectrometers market can be segmented into three main types: Energy Dispersive Type (EDXRF), Wavelength Dispersive Type (WDXRF), and Polarized Energy Dispersive Type (PEDXRF). EDXRF provides rapid elemental analysis by detecting emitted X-rays across a broad energy range, while WDXRF offers higher resolution and sensitivity through precise wavelength selection. PEDXRF enhances signal quality and reduces background noise using polarized X-rays, improving detection limits. Each type serves diverse applications across industries like material science, environmental analysis, and quality control.</p></p>
