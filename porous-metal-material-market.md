<p><strong>The Porous Metal Material Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Independent Hole Type</li><li>Continuous Hole Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The porous metal material market encompasses two main types: independent hole type and continuous hole type. Independent hole type features discrete, isolated pores, allowing for targeted fluid flow and filtration applications. This configuration is ideal for specific tasks, such as air and liquid filtration. In contrast, the continuous hole type consists of interconnected pores, facilitating higher permeability and effective gas or liquid transport. This type is often used in energy applications, including fuel cells and batteries, due to its enhanced efficiency.</p></p>
