<p><p>The pharmaceutical oral glass bottles market includes key types such as low borosilicate glass and soda lime glass. Low borosilicate glass is known for its resistance to thermal shock and chemical stability, making it suitable for storing various medications. In contrast, soda lime glass is cost-effective and widely used for packaging due to its good mechanical properties, although it is less chemically resistant. Both types play crucial roles in ensuring the safe storage and preservation of pharmaceutical products.</p></p>
