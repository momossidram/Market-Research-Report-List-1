<p><strong>The Voltage Indicators Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact</li><li>Non Contact</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Voltage indicators are categorized into two main types: contact and non-contact. Contact voltage indicators require physical contact with the electrical circuit to detect voltage presence, typically using probes or clips. They offer accurate readings and are commonly used in technical applications. Non-contact voltage indicators, on the other hand, detect voltage from a safe distance, using electromagnetic fields, making them safer for quick checks in live environments. Both types serve essential roles in ensuring electrical safety and efficient troubleshooting.</p></p>
