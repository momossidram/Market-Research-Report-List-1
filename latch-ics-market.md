<p><strong>The Latch ICs Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>SR (Set-Reset) Type</li><li>D (Data) Type</li><li>T (toggle) Type</li><li>J-K Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Latch ICs are essential components in digital electronics, serving various functions in data storage and processing. The SR (Set-Reset) latch stores one bit of data, allowing for setting, resetting, or maintaining its state. The D (Data) latch captures input data and holds it until a control signal changes it. The T (Toggle) latch alternates its state with each control signal, useful for counting applications. The J-K latch combines features of SR and T latches, allowing for versatile toggling while preventing invalid states, making it a popular choice in sequential logic circuits.</p></p>
