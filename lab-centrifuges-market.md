<p><p>The lab centrifuge market is segmented into three main types: General, High-Speed, and Super Speed centrifuges. General centrifuges are versatile and suited for routine applications, offering moderate speeds for various sample types. High-speed centrifuges operate at significantly higher speeds, enhancing separation efficiency, ideal for biological and clinical applications. Super speed centrifuges reach even greater velocities, suitable for specialized research tasks, such as isolating subcellular components and pelleting viruses. Each type serves distinct laboratory needs based on performance and application requirements.</p></p>
