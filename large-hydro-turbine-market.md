<p><p>The large hydro turbine market comprises several key types, each suited for specific hydropower applications. Francis turbines are versatile and widely used in medium head hydro plants, offering efficiency across various flow conditions. Kaplan turbines are ideal for low head applications, utilizing adjustable blades for optimal performance. Pelton turbines excel in high head scenarios, converting the kinetic energy of water jets into mechanical energy. Other types include bulbs and cross-flow turbines, catering to diverse geographical and operational requirements in hydropower generation.</p></p>
