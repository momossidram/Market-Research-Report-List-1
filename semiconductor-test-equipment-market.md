<p><ul><li>Wafer Test Equipment</li><li>Packaged Device Test Equipment</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor test equipment market is primarily divided into two types: wafer test equipment and packaged device test equipment. Wafer test equipment is used to evaluate semiconductor wafers before they are diced into individual chips, focusing on electrical performance and reliability. In contrast, packaged device test equipment assesses the performance of individual semiconductor devices after they have been encapsulated, ensuring they meet specifications for functionality and quality. Both types are essential for maintaining the efficiency and reliability of semiconductor manufacturing processes.</p></p>
