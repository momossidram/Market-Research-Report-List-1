<p><p>The antifreeze agent market is categorized into three main types: organic, inorganic, and hybrid antifreeze agents. </p><p>Organic antifreeze agents, typically based on glycols, provide excellent thermal protection and are widely used in automotive applications. Inorganic antifreeze agents, such as salts and alcohols, are often less expensive and suitable for industrial uses. Hybrid antifreeze agents combine properties of both organic and inorganic types, offering enhanced efficiency and compatibility across a range of applications, making them versatile solutions for temperature regulation.</p></p>
