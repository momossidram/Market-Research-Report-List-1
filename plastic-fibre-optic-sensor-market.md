<p><p>The plastic fibre optic sensor market is primarily categorized into two types: Thru-beam and Reflective sensors. Thru-beam sensors consist of a light source and a detector positioned apart, allowing them to detect an object when it interrupts the beam. Reflective sensors, on the other hand, combine the light source and detector in one unit, sensing changes in light intensity due to nearby objects or environmental conditions. Both types find applications in industries like automation, safety, and environmental monitoring.</p></p>
