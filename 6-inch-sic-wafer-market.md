<p><p>The 6-inch SiC wafer market comprises two main types: conductive and semi-insulating SiC wafers. Conductive SiC wafers are primarily used for high-power and high-frequency applications due to their excellent electrical conductivity, making them ideal for power electronics and high-temperature environments. In contrast, semi-insulating SiC wafers, which possess high resistivity, are utilized in high-voltage devices and RF applications to prevent unwanted electrical interference, ensuring efficient device performance and reliability in various electronic applications.</p></p>
