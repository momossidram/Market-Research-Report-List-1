<p><ul><li>PTFE</li><li>FEP</li><li>PFA</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The perfluoropolymer market consists of three primary types: PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene), FEP (fluorinated ethylene propylene), and PFA (perfluoroalkoxy alkane). PTFE is renowned for its exceptional non-stick properties and high heat resistance, making it popular in cookware and industrial applications. FEP offers similar benefits but has better transparency and ease of processing, suitable for wiring insulation. PFA provides enhanced chemical resistance and flexibility, often used in high-purity applications, such as semiconductor manufacturing, due to its superior performance in extreme conditions.</p></p>
