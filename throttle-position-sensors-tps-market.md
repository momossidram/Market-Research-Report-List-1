<p><p>Throttle Position Sensors (TPS) are crucial components in vehicles that monitor the position of the throttle valve, enabling precise control of the engine's air intake. The market for TPS includes two primary types: end switch type and potentiometer type. </p><p>The end switch type features a simple on-off mechanism that indicates when the throttle is fully open or closed, while the potentiometer type provides continuous variable resistance, allowing for more accurate positional feedback across the throttle's range, enhancing engine performance and efficiency.</p></p>
