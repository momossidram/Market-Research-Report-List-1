<p><p>The doughnut market can be primarily categorized into two types: cake style and yeast style. Cake-style doughnuts are denser and made from a batter that is leavened with baking powder, resulting in a rich and flavorful treat, often enjoyed for their unique textures and varieties. In contrast, yeast-style doughnuts are lighter and fluffier, utilizing yeast for leavening, which gives them a delightful rise and chewiness. Both types attract different consumer preferences, contributing to the diverse offerings in the doughnut market.</p></p>
