<p><p>The targeted DNA sequencing market encompasses various techniques aimed at specific genomic regions. Exome sequencing focuses on the protein-coding regions of genes, allowing for the identification of mutations linked to diseases. Enrichment sequencing enhances the detection of rare variants by selectively capturing regions of interest. Amplicon sequencing targets specific gene regions through PCR amplification, offering high sensitivity for small sample sizes. Other methods include various hybridization and capture techniques, catering to diverse research and clinical applications in genomics.</p></p>
