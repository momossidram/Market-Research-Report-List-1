<p><strong>The Metal Complex Dyes Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>1:1 Metal-Complexes</li><li>1:2 Metal-Complexes</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metal complex dyes market is categorized into 1:1 and 1:2 metal-complexes based on the ratio of metal ions to dye molecules. 1:1 metal-complexes consist of one metal ion coordinated to one dye molecule, resulting in stable colors and high performance in textile applications. In contrast, 1:2 metal-complexes feature a single metal ion bound to two dye molecules, enhancing color strength and lightfastness. Both types cater to various industries, including textiles, plastics, and printing, driven by their superior qualities.</p></p>
