<p><p>Thermoelectric modules are devices that convert temperature differences into electrical energy through the Seebeck effect. The market is categorized into two main types: single-stage modules and multistage modules. Single-stage modules feature a single thermoelectric junction and are typically used for applications requiring moderate temperature differentials. In contrast, multistage modules consist of multiple thermoelectric junctions stacked together, enabling greater efficiency and higher temperature differentials, making them ideal for more demanding thermal management tasks and power generation applications.</p></p>
