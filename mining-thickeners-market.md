<p><ul><li>Peripheral Drive</li><li>Center Drive</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Mining thickeners are crucial for solid-liquid separation in mineral processing. Peripheral drive thickeners utilize a motor-driven mechanism located at the periphery of the tank, ensuring efficient sedimentation and minimized torque on the structure. In contrast, center drive thickeners feature a centrally located drive mechanism, offering easier maintenance and a compact design. Both types serve to enhance the concentration of minerals and optimize water recovery, but they differ in design and operational efficiency depending on specific application needs.</p></p>
